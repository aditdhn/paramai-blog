ParamAI started with a simple observation: Indian students get career advice
shaped more by family pressure and dated stereotypes than by evidence. We
built an AI assessment that matches you to **930+ real careers** across
**41 sectors**, grounded in dual RIASEC + OCEAN psychometrics and a knowledge
bank of 860 calibrated career profiles.

This blog is the long-form companion. You’ll find:

- **Sector deep dives.** Honest, research-backed guides to every sector in the
  ParamAI knowledge bank — who thrives there, how to break in, and what the
  realistic paths look like.
- **Assessment science.** The research behind the questions: what RIASEC and
  OCEAN actually measure, why we use adaptive item response theory, and how
  we avoid the failure modes that plague quiz-based "career tests."
- **Student and parent guides.** Practical writing for families making real
  decisions — stream selection, entrance prep, first-job transitions.

No affiliate links. No sponsored rankings. Just the research we use to build
the product.
